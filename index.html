All About Pigs

Ten Facts About Pigs

  1. Piglets open their eyes immediately after they're born.
  2. There are about 675 million pigs in the world.
  3. The scientific name for pigs is sus.
  4. Domestic pigs in the United States eat diets of mostly corn.
  5. Pigs have very poor eyesight.
  6. Pigs are actually very clean animals!
  7. Adult pigs can run at a max speed of 11 miles per hour.
  8. Pigs are very smart animals, too.
  9. Even pigs dream while they sleep.
  10. A domestic pig can live for up to 20 years.

Bonus Fact:


Did you know that the Washington Commanders NFL team's mascot is a pig? His name is Major Tuddy!
